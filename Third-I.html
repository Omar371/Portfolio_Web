THIRD-I

This project is about a device integrated with a hat to assist the visually impaired when they are walking outside.

The project was for a second year electrical design course. The project made up 80% of the course mark and an average of 90% was obtained.

Designed the schematic for circuit board using EAGLE CAD and finalized the final pcb design that was used later to connect all the sensors and actuators with wires and solder them on the pcb.

Used C language and Arduino IDE to integrate the software part with the hardware components and make the overall device able to work.

More information on what does the device do in detail and a demo can be found here: https://devpost.com/software/oubli
